Visual Branding and User Interface Observations

Target's Use of Red in Branding

The red color featured in Target’s branding is highly effective. It immediately catches the viewer’s eye and draws them into the website. This bold use of red creates a strong first impression and enhances the overall appeal of the site.Target website

Concerns with the Current Screenshot

In contrast, the screenshot being reviewed does not achieve the same effect. The site appears oversaturated with yellow tones and washed-out colors, which detract from the visual experience. Instead of engaging the user, these colors make the website look outdated and uninteresting.

Steam's Approach to Interface Design

Steam, on the other hand, excels in maintaining a clean and easily manageable search bar. The color choices on Steam’s site are neither overwhelming nor dull, striking a comfortable balance that supports user navigation. The overall color layout is well executed, avoiding extremes and ensuring that users do not feel the need to adjust their screens while searching the site. Steam website
